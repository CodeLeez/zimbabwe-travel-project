Harare

Zim's Best Brews

Harare is quickly becoming a hidden gem for coffee lovers. The city is home to a growing number of cafés offering a unique blend of locally sourced beans and international brewing techniques.

The local coffee culture is rich and vibrant, with cafés that each offer something special. From cozy spots tucked away in leafy corners to sleek modern spaces with panoramic city views, there’s a café for every mood.

Queen of Hearts Cafe

The Queen of Hearts cafe offers a lovely dining experience in a serene environment. Their chefs prepare a variety of delicious meals using simple and fresh ingredients in a unique open kitchen structure.

Address

1 Hurworth Road, Highlands

What i like about it

They have a great daily cafe menu that offers nutritious & delicious breakfast, authentic Indian curries, mouthwatering gourmet burgers, wholesome & fresh salads & delectable baked goods.Their food tastes amazing!

Cafe Pistachio

Café Pistachio stands out for its delightful breakfast and a warm and welcoming environment.The café's design reflects a blend of contemporary style and local charm, creating a cozy nook where you can unwind.

Address

Shop 19, Sam Levy's Village

What i like about it

They serve an array of breakfast options that cater to various tastes, from traditional Zimbabwean dishes to international favorites.The inviting atmosphere is enhanced by their proffesional and friendly staff.

Boss Cafe

Boss Cafe is a vibrant restaurant in the bustling area of Harare Central. This popular eatery combines a warm and welcoming atmosphere with a diverse menu that caters to both local and international tastes.

Address

60 Bedford Road, King George

What i like about it

It has an impressive selection of beverages including local wines and freshly brewed coffees. It’s an excellent place to relax and has a cozy spot to catch up with friends or family over a meal.You can be sure to have a great time.
